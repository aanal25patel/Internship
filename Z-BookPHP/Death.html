Death: An Inside Story

About Book:

In this unique treatise-like exposition,

Sadhguru dwells extensively upon his inner experience as he expounds the more profound aspects of death that are rarely spoken about.

About Author:
Jaggi Vasudev (born 3 September 1957), known publicly as Sadhguru, is an Indian yogi and author.
Vasudev earned a bachelor's degree in English from the University of Mysore and has been teaching yoga in southern India since 1982.
